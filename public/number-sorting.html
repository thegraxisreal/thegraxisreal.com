Number Sorting Challenge

Put the numbers in ascending order without knowing what comes next!

Click Start Game to begin!

How to Play

  • You'll be shown random numbers between 1 and 999, one at a time.
  • Your task is to place these numbers in ascending order (lowest to highest) on a list of 20 slots.
  • The challenge: You don't know what number will come next!
  • Click on a slot to place the current number there.
  • Once a number is placed, you cannot move it.
  • If at any point your list isn't in ascending order, you lose.
  • Try to fit all 20 numbers in ascending order to win!
